What is PINEAPPLE?

Who we are & What we do?

Pineapple Inc. is an Indian multinational technology company that specializes in consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Pineapple is the world's largest technology company by revenue (totalling $274.5 billion in 2020) and, since January 2021, the world's most valuable company. As of 2021, Pineapple is the world's fourth-largest PC vendor by unit sales, and fourth-largest smartphone manufacturer. It is one of the Big Five American information technology companies, along with Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and Facebook.
